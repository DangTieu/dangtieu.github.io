About Me

Photography for me allows an individual to express their views on the world and how they express their feelings without the use of words. Why I do photography is so I can find a way to communicate my thoughts through the means of art in my photos. Photography for me has always been something fun and to me has not been a hassle to find time for. For me I've never been too good at communicating and could not express myself in the ways that I wish I could. For me socializing is a little hard so through that I found that photos could do things that my own words can't express. Something I also enjoy is that the feedback from people saying they like my work or people who inspire me, it creates a whole new community to get yourself involved in. The photography community is always helpful and keeps you going. So taking photos is my passion for me it creates a communication connect while making it enjoyable for me to work with.
